# Patient — Playbooks

You're a member at a DPC practice that uses MemberMD. The platform is how you talk to your care team, manage your visits and meds, see your labs, and pay for your membership. Everything you can do lives in your patient portal at [`app.membermd.io/#/patient`](/patient).

## Your portal at a glance

- **Dashboard** — quick links + alerts (unsigned consents, balance due, upcoming appointment).
- **Appointments** — book, reschedule, cancel; see your past visits.
- **Health Records** — past encounters, treatments, your provider's notes.
- **Lab Results** — your labs, with trends over time.
- **Messages** — secure conversations with your care team.
- **Care Team** — the people seeing you, with photos + bios.
- **Locations** — where your practice operates (if multi-location).
- **Family** — manage dependents on your plan.
- **Entitlements** — what your plan includes and what you've used this cycle.
- **Billing** — payment methods, invoices, subscription state.
- **Profile / Settings** — your info, notification preferences, two-factor.

## Important reminders

- **This is not for emergencies.** If you have a medical emergency, call 911. Messages and the portal are NOT real-time and don't replace urgent care.
- **HIPAA-protected.** Everything you write to your care team in Messages is encrypted and only visible to you and your practice's staff. We never share your data with insurance companies or third parties without your explicit consent.
- **Logged in as a family member?** Primary subscribers see dependent records by default. Dependents (when they hit a certain age, configurable per practice) get their own login.
- **Mobile-friendly.** The portal works on phone, tablet, and desktop. Save it to your home screen for one-tap access — it acts like an app.
